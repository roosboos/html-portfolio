The Best Movies According to John

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interstellar

This film has me thinking about the nature of our reality to this day.

Blade Runner 2049

Still the best visuals in a movie I have ever seen. Wish I could watch in IMAX one last time.

Scott Pilgrim vs. the World

Everything from the art style to the comedy is top notch. Could watch this movie 100 more times.
